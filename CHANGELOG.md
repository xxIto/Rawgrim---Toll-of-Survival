# Changelog

All notable changes to Rawgrim: The Toll of Survival are documented here.

## 1.0.0 - 2026-05-31

Initial public release candidate.

### Added

- Rest and survival ledger for short rest and long rest.
- Ration and water spending with gritty long-rest supply thresholds.
- Gold-funded long-rest supply option.
- Arcane Resonance tracking for leveled spellcasting.
- GM-only Arcane Overload prompts with Backlash and Contain resolution.
- Cantrip strain roll prompts.
- Material Catalyst requirement and usage die tracking.
- The Marks corruption stage tracking.
- Prosthetic overload on stage transition.
- Equipment Integrity durability die workflow.
- Lingering Injury safe workflow.
- GM Dashboard with urgency sorting, quick actions, and manual overrides.

### Notes

- Verified against Foundry VTT 14.361.
- Requires dnd5e and libWrapper.
